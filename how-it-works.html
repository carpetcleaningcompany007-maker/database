How vetting works

The point is not to hand out badges for nothing. The checks need enough weight to make customers trust the platform.

1. Application submitted

The cleaner completes the application form with business details, service area, methods, and documents.

2. Insurance checked

Public liability insurance is reviewed and logged with an expiry date for renewal reminders.

3. Business reviewed

Website, contact details, company presence, and public facing consistency are reviewed.

4. Service badges assigned

Relevant badges are awarded based on supplied information and manual review.

5. Listing goes live

The cleaner appears in the public directory with profile details and visible trust signals.

6. Renewal cycle

Expiring documents can trigger reminders and temporary suspension until renewed.
